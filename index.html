NBA Math Hoops

NBA Math Hoops is a board game focused on helping kids learn fundamental math skills in a fun way that is directly correlated with basketball. Kids have the chance to play with their favorite NBA and WNBA player's statistics, while also being able to compete in a basketball league with their peers. NBA Math Hoops was created in 1997 by Tim Sheidt and Jim Fina. They eventually signed on with NBA and Hasbro, who signed on as founding partners and donated their rights to Learn Fresh at no cost. Together, they are dedicated to improving student engagement and achievement through the creation and distribution of innovative, fun, and effective educational tools.
